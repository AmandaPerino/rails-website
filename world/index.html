Rails World

Shaping the future of Ruby on Rails.

What is Rails World?

Launched for the 20th anniversary of the Rails framework, Rails World is an annual two-day, two track community conference featuring technical talks, demos, workshops, networking, and keynotes about the latest features and best practices in Rails development.
